Empowering each bird to fly free

Non-profit

A ​Free Bird™ ​is a non­-profit arts education organization. We are looking for enthusiastic individuals who are interested in the arts, therapy, and helping children to make a difference.

We Provide

Artistic resources to hospitals and their patients so these kids can express their ideas and passions, while they go through their treatments. Children are natural artists and show an exceptional talent when given the right guidance.

Our Work

Our work includes providing art tutors to the hospitals and kids, Art Days in the pediatric wards and the children’s museums and special Event Days for kids who finished their treatments.

Commitment

We are committed to helping children keep their spirits strong and never loose hope for their future. All art forms are empowering and we believe we can help the doctors and nurses help these kids.
